The Amazing World of Photosynthesis

June 15, 2023 Dr. Green Thumb 12 Comments Biology

Have you ever wondered how plants make their own food? The process is called photosynthesis, and it's one of nature's most amazing tricks! In this article, we'll explore how plants turn sunlight into energy.

The Photosynthesis Formula

6CO2 + 6H2O + light energy → C6H12O6 + 6O2

This means plants take in carbon dioxide (CO2) and water (H2O), and using sunlight, they convert these into glucose (sugar) and oxygen (O2). The oxygen they release is what we breathe!

Try This at Home!

Watch photosynthesis in action with this simple experiment:

  1. Place a leaf in a clear glass of water
  2. Put it in a sunny spot
  3. Wait a few hours and look for tiny bubbles

Those bubbles are oxygen being released by the leaf!

The Magic of Mathematics: Fibonacci Sequence

June 10, 2023 Professor Numbers 8 Comments Mathematics

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, usually starting with 0 and 1. It appears unexpectedly in nature, art, and architecture.
